Kenyan music

Music is said to be food for the soul.Kenya has a variety of music and musicians with each having his her own unique way of making music.

The different types of music in Kenya are:

    Genge tone
    Kapuka
    Kenyan hip hop
    Rhumba

Kapuka

Kapuka, also reffered to as boomba music is a form of rap music that has hip hop and reggae beats. The lyrics are ussualy in English, Swahili, Sheng or local dialects.

Artists doing kapuka music in Kenya include:

    Nameless
    The late E-Sir
    Nyashinski
